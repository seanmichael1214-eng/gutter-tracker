## üîç Research Framework

### **The 5 Questions**
For every idea, I ask:

1. **What problem does this solve?**
   - Must be specific and painful
   - "I wish I had a tool that..." statements

2. **Who has this problem?**
   - Target audience definition
   - Size of market
   - Willingness to pay

3. **How are they solving it now?**
   - Current solutions
   - Pain points with existing tools
   - Opportunity for improvement

4. **Why AI? Why now?**
   - What makes AI uniquely suited?
   - Recent AI advances that enable this
   - Why competitors haven't done it

5. **Can Sean build and deploy this?**
   - Technical complexity (1-10)
   - Time estimate (hours/days/weeks)
   - APIs and costs required
   - Portfolio value
